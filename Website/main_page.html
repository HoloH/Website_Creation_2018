Bois de Bologne: Around the small lake

by: Ola Nordmann

Region: Paris

Time: <1h

Difficulty: Easy

Start the hike by the small lake in the Bologne forrest. Follow the track around the lake. Watch out for joggers and dogs on the loose. If you're feeling extravagant, take the trip to the lake island and eat at the restaurant. Happy hiking!
